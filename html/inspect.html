MEAT INSPECTION

Meat inspection is of course, very expedient. A very high percentage of the populace consume Meat, and so, this could be a source of infection by pathogenic organisms. Slaughtering of meat must be done under good sanitary conditions, but this rule has been defiled by many, and so care must be taken so that we dont come down with food-borne pathogens.

Also, some animals may have come down with some infection by bacteria, viruses, fungi etc. These infections may have caused lesions in various organs or muscles, and these are the parts of the animal we consume for food.If we consume an infected organ or muscle, we can also become infected by the pathogenic organism, which causes ill-health.

If meat is properly inspected, this occurence would be avoided to a great extent. Both meat gotten from within and those that are imported need to be properly inspected. So, Yes, meat inspection is totally and utterly necessary for the promotion of Public Health.
