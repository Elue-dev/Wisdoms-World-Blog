TUBERCULOUS MEAT

This type of meat is a meat with Tuberculosis with widespread granulomatous nodules. Unfortunately people buy and consume such when there is inadequate meat inspection by veterinary doctors. When you see a meat looking like this, never buy or consume it, no amount of cooking can make it safe for consumption.

Show this to whoever gets your meat so as to be guided when purchasing them. They must never buy a meat like this as it is detrimental to your health. The people who sell this type of meat usually sell it at a cheaper rate so as to persuade people to buy it. They actually know it is not fit for consumption but they sell it anyway. Be careful. We hope this helps. Health is wealth, stay healthy.
